Types of Coffee

Espresso

Strong and concentrated base coffee brewed under pressure.

Latte

Espresso with steamed milk and light foam on top.

Cappuccino

Equal parts espresso, steamed milk, and milk foam.

Americano

Espresso diluted with hot water for a smoother taste.

Mocha

Espresso combined with chocolate and steamed milk.

Flat White

Velvety microfoam milk blended with rich espresso.

Macchiato

Espresso topped with a small amount of milk foam.

Affogato

Espresso poured over vanilla ice cream.
