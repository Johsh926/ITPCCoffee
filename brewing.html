Brewing & Preparation Guide

~ Pour Over

Produces a clean and bright cup. Best for Arabica beans.

~ French Press

Full-bodied and rich. Ideal for medium to dark roasts.

~ Espresso

Concentrated and bold. Base for latte and cappuccino.

~ Cold Brew

Steeped 12–24 hours. Smooth and low acidity.

~ AeroPress

Quick and versatile brewing method with balanced flavor.

~ Moka Pot

Stovetop brewing with strong espresso-like taste.
